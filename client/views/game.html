Welcome to RaiseMeUp!

Here are some instructions for you to get started:

Objective:
Place cubes onto the grid to build stairs that helps the "circular man" escape from the rising water.

Controls:
To place a cube - double click on the grid
To rotate the view - shift + click
To make "circular man" move - keyboard "W", "A", "S", "D"

*Note: Cubes are limited! Make sure you lead the "circular man" to earn cubes by reaching for reward coins!
